Johann Mai

PHOTOGRAPHY

A traveller, a nature lover,
and a photographer

So far - life is a journey. It goes up and down, with falling and rising again.

Being 40 years old and working for 15 years as an engineer in a big German company developing technical products, I finally found something, what I was always searching for. It is freedom and endless opportunities in creating pictures of things I see on our beautiful planet.

I respect engeneering - my problems are the limititations given by physical principles and other boundaries. But photography has been the eye opener. The are no limitations for moments. It is on us to appriciate this and capture them.
